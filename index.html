Branded Residence Partner

Branded Residence Partner is an independent advisory firm specialising in branded residence partnerships and pre-launch investment advisory in India. We work closely with Indian real estate developers to connect them with leading global hospitality, lifestyle, and fashion brands, enabling the creation of distinctive, high-value residential and commercial developments.

We support developers during the Pre-Launch and Expression of Interest (EOI) stages, helping strengthen market positioning, enhance liquidity, and build early sales momentum.
